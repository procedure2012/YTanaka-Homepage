Welcome! I am a PhD candidate at the **University of Tokyo Quantum Information Lab**, working on quantum computing and quantum information. My research focuses on developing practical quantum algorithms and error correction techniques for near-term quantum devices.

## Academic Background

- **Ph.D. in Physics** (2023 – Present)  
  University of Tokyo

- **B.Sc.** (2019 – 2023)  
  Kyoto University  
  Graduated in March 2023

## Research Interests

- **Quantum Error Correction** – Developing resource-efficient surface codes and fault-tolerant architectures
- **Variational Quantum Algorithms** – Hybrid classical-quantum optimization methods for near-term devices
- **Quantum Machine Learning** – Benchmarking and designing quantum kernels for machine learning tasks

## Research Experience

- **Research Intern** at **IBM Quantum** (June 2025 – Present)
- **Research Intern** at **RIKEN Center for Quantum Computing** (April 2024 – October 2024)
