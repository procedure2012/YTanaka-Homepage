Presented at QIP 2024, this work proposes VQE-Anneal, a hybrid approach that integrates Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE) with quantum annealing techniques. The method demonstrates improved convergence and solution quality for combinatorial optimization problems on near-term quantum hardware.
